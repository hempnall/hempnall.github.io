--- layout: post title: Configuring R on Mac OS x date: '2014-06-01T12:02:00.001-07:00' author: James Hook tags: modified_time: '2014-06-01T12:02:11.368-07:00' blogger_id: tag:blogger.com,1999:blog-5482187274444893467.post-9179836440916245471 blogger_orig_url: http://computer-says-yes.blogspot.com/2014/06/configuring-r-on-mac-os-x.html visible: 1 --- You need to download gfortran from the R website then a configure statement like this worked (with a few warnings) 


./configure r_arch=x86_64 F77="gfortran -arch x86_64" FC="gfortran -arch x86_64" --with-system-zlib --x-includes=/opt/X11/include --x-libraries=/opt/X11/lib

The warnings... 


configure: WARNING: you cannot build PDF versions of the R manuals
configure: WARNING: you cannot build PDF versions of vignettes and help pages
