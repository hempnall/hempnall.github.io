If you're cautious, do a test run: (ROBOCOPY can delete data!!)

robocopy [source] [dest] /MIR /L

The /L  switch tells robocopy to "list only".

Then, once you're satisfied, let it go for real by removing the /L switch..
robocopy [source] [dest] /MIR
